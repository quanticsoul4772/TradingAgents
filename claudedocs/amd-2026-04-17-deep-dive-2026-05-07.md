007 bull_case_priced_in score expresses numerically:

| Score signal | PM prose echo |

This is the cleanest L-8 sub-pattern 2 case in the corpus: **PM commits
STRICTER than Hold (Underweight, not just Hold), with explicit reasoning
that mirrors the bull-priced-in mechanism in PM's own words**.

Spec 003 percentile (72.4) is below the 80 threshold AND uses sector
baseline (no per-ticker history) — Spec 003 would NOT have fired even
in a bullish-pre scenario. Yet PM picked the same direction the
mechanism would have suggested.

### F-2: NEW empirical sub-pattern — bull-fully-priced-in + bear-thesis-fresh

Prior PR #41 sweep identified 3 sub-patterns:
1. PM Hold + bull-priced-in scores high
2. PM stricter-than-Hold + bull-priced-in scores high
3. PM Hold + bear-priced-in scores high (kernel for Hybrid D)

AMD-04-17 surfaces a NEW sub-pattern (sub-pattern 4 candidate):

**Sub-pattern 4: Asymmetric — bull fully priced in (`bull_score ≥ 0.80`)
AND bear thesis still ahead (`bear_score ≤ 0.40`) AND PM commits
bearishly (UW or Sell)**.

This is OPPOSITE to the both-sides-priced-in framing that motivated
Hybrid D. Sub-pattern 4 represents asymmetric setups where:
- Bull thesis is exhausted (high bull_score)
- Bear thesis still has runway (low bear_score)
- PM correctly commits to the side with fresh edge

A future Hybrid E filter could key on
`bull_score - bear_score > T_asymmetric` to catch this cohort
explicitly. Cohort too small for retrospective today (n=1 candidate);
record for tracking.

### F-3: Calendar boost INERT at 18d to earnings (correct behavior)

Days to earnings = 18, outside the boost window (14d default).
calendar_boost=0 → effective_bull = base = 0.85. This is the EXPECTED
behavior per spec 008 SC-001 (boost engages only inside window).

The PM prose explicitly says "executing the bulk of the reduction
before the May 5 Q1 2026 earnings report" — PM is operationally
aware of the earnings date even when the spec 008 calendar boost is
mechanically inert. **Calendar awareness is in the PM's reasoning,
not just the spec 008 boost mechanism**. This is a third behavioral-
additive insight: PM has internalized calendar-aware contrarian logic
even when the spec 008 mechanism doesn't apply at the current days-out.

### F-4: A3 momentum filter inverted-condition correctness

AMD was UP 42% in the prior 30 trading days. A3 fires on tickers DOWN
>5% (mean-reversion zone) with UW pre-rating to suppress to Hold. AMD
is the opposite condition — strongly UP — so A3 correctly did NOT
fire (but recorded `fired: None` rather than `fired: False` in this
log; minor instrumentation question, not a bug).

This is the kind of regime where Hybrid C / spec 007 bear-side filters
WOULD theoretically catch the "ticker up massively + earnings approaching"
pattern via a forward-looking mechanism, but AMD-04-17 shows Spec 007
bull at 0.85 and bear at 0.40 — the bull-priced-in side is the
operative one, NOT a both-sides-priced-in or sector-rotation pattern.
